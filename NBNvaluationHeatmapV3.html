NBN Valuations ($B) - for Joint Committee Inquiry into NBN Business Case

Datapoints: Impact at Yr 20, undiscounted to current $$; no interest or inflation included.
Each point is a datarun at: Observable Notebook v5a (Final), calculating the value of the NBN under different assumptions (scenarios).
These graphs visualise the model presented as a submission to the above Inquiry (30.3.2018).
These six graphs show 6 * 6 * 6 (216) scenarios of the model.
Blue indicates positive value. Red/Orange indicates negative or poor value.
Tool: Plot.ly. All code visible, if you select View Source.

Cite: Ferrers, R. What is the value of Australia's National Broadband Network (NBN) - 2021 - 2041? figshare.
Online at: https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.6030926.
